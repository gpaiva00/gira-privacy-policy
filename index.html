Privacy Policy — Gira

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Gira is a randomiser app. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data (custom lists, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults and never transmitted.

Advertising

Gira uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised ads, including:

  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • App usage data

You can limit ad personalisation at any time in your device's Privacy & Security → Tracking settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party SDKs

The only third-party SDK used is Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). No analytics, crash reporting, or other SDKs are included.

Children

Gira is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children. AdMob is configured without child-directed treatment flags.
